Meet Our Dermatologists

Our board certified Dallas dermatologists and licensed esthetician are dedicated to the care of our patients. Dr. James H. Herndon, MD founded the Dermatology Center of Dallas and specializes in cosmetic procedures for improving wrinkles, pigmentation, and other signs of aging. Dr. Peter D. Hino, MD joined the Dermatology Center of Dallas in 1989 and specializes in teenage acne, cutaneous malignancies, and anti-aging products and procedures.

Dr. Jeri Beth Foshee, MD joined the Dermatology Center of Dallas in 2009 and practices medical and cosmetic dermatology. Candace C. Fortson, M.A., C.A., RMT has been a Dermatological Medical Assistant since 1989 and is an advanced laser technician certified in a full range of regenerative procedures and skin care treatments.
